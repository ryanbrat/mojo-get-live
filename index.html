The Mojo story

It's shred-tastic

Mojo started has a hobby, then became a lifestyle.

Late in 2010 I purchased Mojo Cycling. The shop is great, but takes a lot of time. It is easy to get alienated from the sport that I love because I’m always in the shop. To stay as close to the local biking community as possible I work with the local Boy Scouts to build & maintain local trails. I also ride 3 times a week with riders who are on the POD e-mail list. Mojo Cycling also hosts the bi-annual Cycle Therapy event, a day-long hands-on mountain bike riding techniques class. I also ride with my wife & sons as frequently as I can. These activities help me to “keep it real.” ﻿ So, swing by the shop some time to say “hi.” You won’t get any attitude in our shop. We just love to meet people who love to ride. It doesn’t matter if you’re a sponsored rider or a beginner. If you’re out riding—instead of sitting on the sofa—you’re a winner! Plus, I’m always excited to hear about other rider’s love of bikes & cycling.﻿
